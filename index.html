Why isn’t the Internet Green?

Carbon Footprint

The Internet accounts for 3.7% of
global greenhouse emissions.

Central Servers

100 gigabytes of data stored at central
servers results in 0.2 tons of CO2.

Power Consumption

The Internet uses 307
gigawatts annually.
